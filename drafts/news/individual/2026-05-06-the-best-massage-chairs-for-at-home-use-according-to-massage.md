CNN Underscored published a roundup in early May 2026 identifying what it calls the best massage chairs for at-home use, with input from licensed massage therapists. The piece is notable because it pulls in professional perspective rather than relying solely on spec sheets, making it one of the more credible consumer-facing roundups from a major media outlet in recent memory.

## What Was Announced

CNN Underscored, the product review vertical of CNN, consulted massage therapists to evaluate massage chairs for home use. According to the piece, the review is framed around practical at-home self-care rather than clinical treatment, and the therapists interviewed acknowledged upfront that no massage chair fully replicates a hands-on professional session. The full list of specific models reviewed was not included in the story summary available to MCF at publication time, but the roundup appears to include chairs across a range of price points, with Inner Balance cited as one of the brands covered.

Specifications, pricing details, and the complete list of reviewed models are pending verification from the source article directly.

## What This Means for Buyers

Third-party validation from a publication like CNN Underscored carries real weight in the massage chair market, where most online coverage is either thinly disguised affiliate content or brand-produced material. When licensed massage therapists are involved in evaluating chairs, they tend to prioritize different things than spec-focused reviewers: pressure depth and consistency, body coverage, ergonomics for people with tension in specific muscle groups, and how well a chair handles the transition between techniques.

That professional lens is useful context for buyers, but it also has limits worth understanding. Massage therapists evaluate feel and therapeutic logic. They are generally not evaluating long-term reliability, warranty support, or what happens when a roller motor fails two years into ownership. Those factors matter enormously in the $2,000 to $8,000 range where most serious home chairs compete.

Inner Balance is a brand that sits in the mid-range of the home massage chair market. If their chairs made a credible third-party list alongside higher-end competitors, that is worth noting for buyers who are price-sensitive but still want a chair with some therapeutic credibility.
